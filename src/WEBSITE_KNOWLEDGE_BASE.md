## 6. Returns & Refunds

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase.

*   **Return Window:** 30 Days from the date of delivery.
*   **Condition:** Items must be unused, in original packaging, with all tags attached.
*   **Refund Process:**
    1.  Initiate return via "My Profile" > "Orders".
    2.  Print the prepaid shipping label.
    3.  Drop off the package.
    4.  Refund processed to original payment method within 5-7 business days after inspection.
*   **Non-Returnable:** Underwear, customized items, and digital downloads.

## 7. How It Works (User Guide)

1.  **Sign Up:** Create an account using your email. You can upload a custom profile picture.
2.  **Browse:** Use the search bar or category filters to find products. Use "Grid View" or "List View" for convenience.
3.  **Cart:** Add items to your cart. You can review and adjust quantities.
4.  **Checkout:** Proceed to secure checkout. (Note: In this demo version, checkout is simulated and does not charge real money).
5.  **Track:** View your order status in "My Orders".
